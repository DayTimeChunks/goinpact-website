inPact
at a Glance

inPact's is an interactive and participatory platform for project funding and support in the developing world.

Through crowdfunding and blog pages, community members like you select, support and fund small to medium projects where your expertise can be a catalyst for change.

By bridging funders, expertise and collective know-how with local knowledge and ideas you will help empower communities where these projects are born.




How it works



1. Select a project

Find out which projects are ready to go!



2. Support

Decide how to support. Either by becoming an active community member to help projects succeed or by supporting the project directly with financing.



3. Share with friends

Connect your projects to your social media, discuss its progress with others and share your and your project's success.



4. Follow-up

Track your project's progress by logging into myInpact space.



Project Holders

Projet holders are organizations or individuals with a project idea and currently being supported by the inPact community. Support can happen via project financing, know-how or both. Do you have a project idea? Then share your project and get support!




Local Partners

Local partners are key to the success of any project. Being an active member in the community, while having a deep sense of the cultural context are two defining traits of a local change agent. Are you a catalyst for change?




Reviewer Community

We believe that anyone can contribute, also with knowledge! We receive project applications from all over the world and your expertise can help a project holder succeed. Are you willing to help? Contact us to become a part of our reviewer community.




Donors & Investors

Support project holders through donations & investment. The projects that you find on the site have already been appraised and validated by our team. We only publish projects from verified partners that we trust.




The inPact Team

With this initiative, our goal is to promote great opportunities for those in need. With access to the right resources, people can become empowered by their own abilities and gain the confidence to fulfil their potential. Learn more about our work by getting in touch with our team today.
