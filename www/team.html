Know more about inPact

inPact is a peer to peer lending and giving platform empowering people in development projects. Our objective is to enhance collaboration and cooperation in a fair and transparent way.



OUR STORY...

Everything started in 2016, with a group of friends dreaming about changing the world. We all share common values and want to make the world a better place. Through our vision of building a collective platform to connect people around the world willing to share their experience, their networks and their support.

After a year of preparation, we decided to officially start our journey and registered as a non-profit in France, under the name of ADaPPT. The journey has been very exciting and we are now expanding our network of local partners and building our first projects.

Come along with us on a great venture!





Vision

Our vision is to create a worldwide community that through collaboration allows any citizen of the world the opportunity to achieve their local development projects.



Mission

Our mission is to identify local entrepreneurs and change makers in developing countries, offering exposure of their ideas to individuals interested in providing financial support or expertise.



Approach

We provide a crowdfunding platform where you can donate and invest in local development projects that are both appraised and financed by the inPact community.



The inPact Team

Our team is a mixture of backgrounds and cultures and live in different places in Europe. Despite of the distance between us, the dream of this project and its values guides our collaboration. We are striving, together with you, to make the inPact venture a success!
